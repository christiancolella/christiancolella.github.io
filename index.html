About

Hi! I'm MelonBP. I'm a game developer who enjoys creating captivating experiences inside Minecraft. I started playing Minecraft in 2011, and I have always been inspired by the endless possibilities that the game offers. Since 2019, I have been publishing maps to Java Realms. More recently, in 2021, I have partnered with Microsoft to publish to the Minecraft Marketplace. I'm super grateful for the opportunity to share my maps with these audiences. This website is a showcase of the content that I have produced for both the Marketplace and Realms.
