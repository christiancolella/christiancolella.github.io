PvBee

PvBee is a minigame in which any number of players (although Realms has a 10 player online limit) must defend 20 waves of bees. Each wave lasts 90 seconds, and players can upgrade their gear and purchase other items in the in-game shop between waves. Players receive a honeycomb for every bee they kill, and these honeycombs are used as the currency for shop items.

There are 7 different types of bees, and these variations inflict a unique potion effect (e.g. poison, nausea, blindness) upon stinging a player. Waves become progressively more difficult, featuring more powerful bees and in larger swarms.

Players can choose between 4 game difficulties, which affect the maximum health of the player.
