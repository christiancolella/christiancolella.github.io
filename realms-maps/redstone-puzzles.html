Redstone Puzzles

Redstone Puzzles is a minigame in which the player solves redstone-based challenges. There are 24 puzzles in total, each increasing in difficulty as the player progresses. The map assumes no prior redstone knowledge, and the puzzles provide intuition for how each redstone component functions.
